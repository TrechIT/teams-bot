Synthesis: Move Study To Pending
================================

ID: 4
Problem / Description:
If a user on synthesis requests a study to be sent to pending because it is currently in synthesis' completed status without a report follow this guide

Resolution / Steps:
Log into SynthesisNavigate to Exams > Complete Identify the correct study with the requesting user take note of the EXAM IDopen a ticket with Synthesis (email support@synthesis.health)They should resolve the ticket in about 30-60 minutesOnce you get confirmation from Synthesis let the end user know it has been resolvedClose the ticket
